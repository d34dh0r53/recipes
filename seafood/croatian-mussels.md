Croatian mussels
================

Sweat:

- 3 cloves garlic
- 2 tablespoon olive oil

Combine:

- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs

Add, deglaze and cover until mussels open, 5-7 min:

- 2 pounds mussels
- 1 cup white wine

Garnish and serve in the pan:

- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Serve with:

- French bread, warmed
