Turkey
=======

- Brine (12-72 hours):
  - 1 part salt
  - 20 parts water
- Meat: turkey
- Slather: olive oil
- Rub:
  - 1 part salt
  - 2 parts pepper
- Warm up: 30 minutes
- Wood: apple, hickory, or oak
- Smoker temperature: 265 F
- Internal temperature: 165 F
- Rested temperature: 140 F

Approximate cook times:

- Thighs: 1.5 hours
- Breasts: 1.5 hours
