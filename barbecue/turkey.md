Turkey
=======

Smoke for 2.5 - 3 hours.

- Brine (12-72 hours):
  - 1 part salt
  - 20 parts water
- Meat: turkey thighs
- Slather: olive oil
- Rub:
  - 1 part salt
  - 2 parts pepper
- Warm up: 30 minutes
- Wood: apple, hickory, or oak
- Smoker temperature: 265 F
- Internal temperature: 165 F
- Rested temperatur: 140 F
