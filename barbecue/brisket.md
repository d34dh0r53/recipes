Brisket
=======

> It's not possible to write a traditional "recipe" for brisket. Instead, we have the variables.

- Grade: high choice or prime
- Rub:
  - 1 part salt
  - 1 part pepper
- Warm up: 1 hour
- Smoker temperature: 225 F
- Wood: mesquite
- Internal temperature: 190 F
- Wrap: naked
- Rested temperature: 140 F
